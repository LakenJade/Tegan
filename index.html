Isn't it amazing how one image can tell such a story? The images I have collected over the years tell my story. It is the story of Tegan Leigh Thomas, a girl who is high on life, a girl with a thirst for adventure. Now thanks to my dearest friend Laken I can share this adventure with you. Welcome to my world, I hope you enjoy your stay.


I make no claims that I am perfect, but I am comfortable in my own skin. I love to decorate myself, put on my war paint, my armour, then head out to battle so to speak. I love to dance, I love to sing (even though those around me probably wish I wouldn't), and I love music. The world is my stage, and I'm loving every minute of it.


In my career as a dancer and model I have had the honour of working with so many wonderful artists. These amazing friends have assisted me in my quest to live my life to the fullest and this page is a tribute to every single one of them. To all those who have contributed to the photographic story book of my journey: I salute you, thank you.


I believe life is too short to wear the same outfit twice. I constantly reinvent my entire appearance to become a better representation of who I am on the inside as I adapt to challenges on my life path. They say you only live once, I say you only die once… you live quite a bit actually. Some people misinterpret my constantly changing persona as an identity crisis, some even call me a wannabe, but in truth I grow as a person every day. Being the same "old Tegan" day in and day out seems dull to me.


I am a geek, a diva, a gamer, a lady, a friend, a lover… I am a woman of many faces. Here I will share myself with you via my pictures and my blog in hopes that it will inspire someone… somewhere… TO BE AWESOME LIKE ME — Tegan Leigh Thomas
